Return & Exchange Policy

Returns

If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 30 days for a full refund. Please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our customer support team to initiate the return process.
  2. Package the item securely and include a copy of your original invoice.
  3. Ship the package to our return address, which will be provided by our customer support team.
  4. Once we receive your return, we will process your refund within 5 business days.

Exchanges

If you received a defective or incorrect item, we will gladly exchange it for you. Follow these steps:

  1. Contact our customer support team to request an exchange.
  2. Package the item securely and include a copy of your original invoice.
  3. Ship the package to our exchange address, which will be provided by our customer support team.
  4. Once we receive your item, we will send out the replacement within 3 business days.

Conditions

Please note that the following conditions apply to returns and exchanges:

  • Items must be in their original packaging and in unused condition.
  • Returns and exchanges must be initiated within 30 days of the purchase date.
  • Shipping costs for returns and exchanges are the responsibility of the customer, unless the item is defective or incorrect.
  • We reserve the right to refuse returns or exchanges that do not meet our policy guidelines.

This return and exchange policy is subject to change without notice. Please check back for updates.
